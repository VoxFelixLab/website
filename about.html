Our Story

About
VOX Felix.

We are a 501(c)(3) research organization investigating how AI ethics can move beyond principles and become something measurable, auditable, and ultimately verifiable.

Origin

Why VOX Felix Exists

Most AI organizations are building models. Very few are asking who will verify that those systems behave ethically, transparently, and responsibly once deployed.

VOX Felix Laboratory Corporation was created to explore that question — and to build the institutional infrastructure to answer it. We are a research laboratory and fellowship community, not a vendor or advocacy organization. Our findings are independent. Our research is public. Our mission is accountability.

Distinction

What Makes Us Different

Principle

Independent

We are a nonprofit research organization with no commercial product to sell and no vendor relationship to protect. Our conclusions follow the evidence.

Principle

Measurable

We believe ethics must be operationalizable — not just aspirational. We build frameworks that can be audited, tested, and verified in the real world.
