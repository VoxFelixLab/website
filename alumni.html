What the Fellowship Means

The VOX Felix fellowship is not a credential — it is a commitment. Alumni of VOX Felix have worked directly on questions that shape how AI is governed, deployed, and experienced by real people in real communities. That work does not end when a fellowship term concludes.

We maintain active relationships with our alumni community, connect alumni to emerging opportunities, and welcome alumni contributions to ongoing research and initiatives.
